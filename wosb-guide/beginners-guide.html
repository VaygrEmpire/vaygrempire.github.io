    NEVER SELL THESE RESOURCES

    Battle Marks, Voodoo Skulls, and Escudos are extremely rare currencies used for Imperial ships and endgame content. While they sell for significant gold, you cannot rebuy themand will desperately need them later.

    First 10 Things to Do

    1
    Complete Tutorial Quests

    Follow the port quest NPC to unlock your first ship, Horizont, and learn basic mechanics.

    2
    Unlock Fast Travel & Escort Skills

    These two skills are mandatory for efficient gameplay. Prioritize them immediately.

    3
    Build Your First Mine

    Find the closest Iron Mine to your spawn and build there. Iron is essential for all progression.

    4
    Add a Wood Mine

    Wood is your second mine. Iron + Wood are the foundation of your economy.

    5
    Save for Blackwind Ship

    Skip La Salamandre entirely. Go straight from Horizont to Blackwind for the combat line.

    6
    Build at Faction Discount Ports

    Check each ship's faction flag and travel to ports with that faction for 25% discount.

    7
    Create Personal Island at Level 9

    Unlock at rank 9. Place it near black waters (accessible at level 12) for better resources.

    8
    First Island Building: Workshop

    Build the Plate Workshop first (only costs gold). Use it to craft plates for other buildings.

    9
    Complete 5 Buildings Before Level 20

    Building costs increase at levels 11 and 21. Finish core buildings before hitting level 20.

    10
    Start Trading North to South

    Buy Sugar/Salt in north ports, sell in south ports. This is the best early gold farming method.

    Personal Island Setup

    Island Location Strategy

    Before Level 12: Build your island near your spawn location with 1 Iron mine and 1 Wood mine nearby. This is temporary.

    After Level 12: Relocate your island and mines to Black Waters region. Black water mines produce better resources, and you can access them starting at level 12.

    Relocation Process: You don't "move" the island â€” you just build a new island in black waters and demolish old mines. Move all resources to your new port/island.

    Mine Placement (7,000 Range Rule)

    All your mines must be within 7,000 distance of your Personal Island for auto-collection. Press

    Where to Build Ships (25% Discount)

    Every ship in the game has a faction flag shown on its info page. If you build that ship at a port controlled by the matching faction, you get a 25% resource discount. This saves enormous amounts of materials over time.

    How to Find Faction Discount Ports:

    1. 1. Click on the ship you want to build (top-left ship menu)
    2. 2. Look at the faction flag icon (it shows which faction gives the discount)
    3. 3. Open the world map and find ports controlled by that faction
    4. 4. Carry your resources to that faction's port
    5. 5. Build your ship and save 25% on all materials
    Pro Tip: Transport resources to the discount port before building. The 25% savings is significant â€” it can mean 100+ iron, wood, or other materials saved per ship.

    How to Get Resources Efficiently

    Mining (Passive Income)

    Your Personal Island mines generate resources passively. Auto-collect from your island every few hours.

    Ship Dismantling

    Capture NPC ships, bring them to port, and dismantle them for Beams and other resources. Much cheaper than crafting Beams early game.

    Fisherman Village

    Trade fish to Fisherman Villages for iron, wood, and other materials.

    Merchant at Sea

    Find floating merchants in the ocean who sell plates, bulkheads, and beams for gold.

    NPC Ship Looting

    Sink NPC ships and loot their cargo. Press
    E
    to see what they carry before attacking.

    Trading House

    Buy resources from other players at Trading Houses in major ports. Prices fluctuate based on supply and demand.
