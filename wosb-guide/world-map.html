    Main Regions of the Map

    Ports and Harbors

    Ports are the economic and social hubs of World of Sea Battle. Every port is controlled by a faction and serves as a safe haven for captains to dock, repair ships, recruit crew, and conduct business. Major ports like Port Royale, Tortuga, and Nassau are legendary trading centers where fortunes are made and alliances forged.

    Within ports, captains can access shipyards for construction and upgrades, warehouses for storing valuable cargo, and taverns for recruiting special crew members. Port markets fluctuate based on supply and demand, creating opportunities for shrewd traders to exploit price differences across regions.

    Mines and Resource Nodes

    Scattered throughout the world are resource-rich mines that provide passive income for those who control them. These include sawmills, iron mines, copper deposits, coal veins, and rum distilleries. Once constructed and maintained, these facilities generate resources automatically, fueling your empire's growth.

    However, mines located in high-danger zones are frequently contested by rival factions and pirate crews. Controlling strategic resource nodes can shift the balance of power in faction warfare, making them prime targets for raids and sieges.

    Personal Islands

    Every captain dreams of owning their own island â€” a private stronghold where they can build, produce, and store resources without interference. Each player can own up to three personal islands, unlocked through progression milestones and strategic investments.

    Islands serve multiple purposes: production hubs for crafting materials, secure storage for treasure and supplies, and military bases for launching expeditions. Smart captains position their islands near safe harbors for easy trade access while keeping military outposts in contested zones to support faction operations.

    Altars and Mystical Sites

    Ancient altars appear throughout the ocean, shrouded in mystery and magic. These sacred sites allow captains to exchange Escudo(premium currency) for rare and valuable resources such as legendary crew contracts, exotic ship blueprints, and powerful voodoo charms.

    Altars are often found in remote, high-danger zones, requiring brave captains to navigate treacherous waters and fend off aggressive NPCs. Some altars are rumored to grant blessings that enhance ship performance or unlock hidden quests tied to the lore of the Voodoo Cult faction.

    Fishing Villages

    Small fishing villages dot the coastlines and remote islands, offering an alternative to the crowded markets of major ports. These humble settlements sell assorted resources at cheaper prices, making them ideal for captains looking to save gold while stocking up on essentials.

    However, fishing villages have limited inventory and do not offer advanced services like ship construction or crew academies. They are best used for quick resupply runs or opportunistic trading during long voyages through dangerous waters.
