    Detailed Progression Guides

    Where to Build Ships (25% Discount)

    Every ship has a faction flag shown on its info page. Building at a port controlled by the matching faction gives you a 25% resource discount. This saves enormous amounts of materials over time.

    How to Find Faction Discount Ports:

    1. 1. Click on the ship you want to build (top-left ship menu)
    2. 2. Look at the faction flag icon on the ship's page
    3. 3. Open the world map and find ports with that faction's flag
    4. 4. Transport your resources to that faction's port
    5. 5. Build the ship and save 25% on all materials
    Example: If Blackwind requires 500 iron, building at a faction discount port only costs 375 iron. That's 125 iron saved â€” enough to craft multiple other items.

    Understanding Ship Statistics

    Durability

    Total hit points and structural integrity. Higher durability means surviving longer under fire and enduring storms.

    Speed

    Maximum sailing velocity and acceleration. Faster ships control engagement range and escape dangerous situations.

    Maneuverability

    Turning radius and responsiveness. Critical for dodging fire, positioning broadsides, and navigating tight waters.

    Crew Capacity

    Maximum sailors aboard. Larger crews improve repair speed, boarding strength, and cannon efficiency.

    Cargo Hold

    Storage capacity for resources, loot, and supplies. Essential for trade runs and long expeditions.

    Cannon Slots

    Number of cannons mountable on each broadside. More cannons = higher damage potential but slower reloads.

    Choosing Your Ship

    Combat Focus: Choose Combat or Heavy branches for maximum firepower and durability. Perfect for PvP battles, bounty hunting, and faction warfare.

    Trade & Economy: Transport branch offers the best cargo capacity for profitable trade routes and resource gathering.

    Speed & Mobility: High-Speed branch excels at reconnaissance, quick strikes, and evading danger - ideal for solo players.

    Siege Warfare: Siege branch is essential for attacking forts, ports, and coastal structures during faction conflicts.

    Prestige & Power: Imperial ships offer unique abilities and superior stats but require special currencies earned through dedicated gameplay.
