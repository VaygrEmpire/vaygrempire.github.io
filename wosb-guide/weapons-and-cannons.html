    Weapons and Cannons

    Overview

    Cannons and artillery define your combat role in World of Sea Battle. From light swivel guns to massive siege mortars, your weapon loadout determines your tactical options in intense naval warfare. Master the characteristics of each weapon class to dominate in PvP battles, guild wars, and territorial sieges.

    Weapons are classified by weight, type, and rank. Light weapons offer rapid-fire capability, while heavy cannons deliver devastating penetration at the cost of reload speed. Bombards provide multi-projectile volleys, and special weapons unlock unique tactical advantages.

    Crafting & Acquisition

    Weapons can be purchased with gold or pirate tokens, or crafted using resources gathered from your personal island and trade routes.

    Combat Strategy

    • 1.
      Match Weapons to Ship Class:

      Light cannons for brigs and sloops, medium for frigates, heavy for men-o'-war. Mismatched loadouts reduce effectiveness.

    • 2.
      Bombards for Alpha Strikes:

      Multi-barrel weapons excel in single devastating volleys. Use for ambush tactics or finishing crippled enemies.

    • 3.
      Mortars for Siege Warfare:

      Essential for port bombardment and area denial. Position behind frontlines and rain destruction on static targets.

    • 4.
      Upgrade Evenly:

      Balance port and starboard armaments. Uneven upgrades leave vulnerabilities in turning engagements.

    Summary

    Weapon selection defines your combat identity. From rapid-fire light cannons to devastating Imperial Bombards, master your arsenal to dominate the seas. Craft strategically, upgrade wisely, and unleash overwhelming firepower upon your enemies.
