    Recruitment Locations

    Taverns

    Pirate Taverns

    Located in pirate towns and captured cities. Recruit Pirates crew.

    City Taverns

    Free or faction cities. Recruit Sailors and Military crew.

    Private Island Taverns

    Build on your island for better encounter rates. All crew types available.

    At Sea

    Adventurers Only

    All 9 Adventurer crew members are ONLY found at sea, never in taverns.

    With Explorer Crew

    +5% chance to find unique crew at sea (Pirates/Sailors/Military, but not Adventurers).

    Boarding NPCs

    Capture crew when boarding. Higher difficulty modes = better chances.

    Strategic Tips

    Combat-Focused Build

    Combine Military crew for maximum firepower and damage control.

    • Gunner: +5% weapon reload speed
    • Commander: +20% gun aiming speed
    • Artillerist: +25% mortar aiming speed
    • Watchman: +40% fire extinguishing
    • Armorer: 20% faster reload after volley

    Trading & Cargo Build

    Maximize cargo capacity and trading efficiency with Sailors.

    • Supercargo: +30% lower overload penalty
    • First Mate: +0.25% speed per sailor
    • Pilot: -33% turning penalty in travel
    • Skipper (Pirate): +0.5% cargo per boarder

    Boarding Build

    Dominate boarding actions with Pirates crew.

    • Harpooner: +60% grappling hook reload
    • Doctor: +30% boarding survivability
    • Senior Physician: +20% crew healing
    • Scout (Adventurer): +30% prisoners
    • Recruiter: +10 crew pickup limit

    Gathering & Exploration Build

    Maximize resource collection with Adventurers and Pirates.

    • Explorer (Adventurer): +5% unique crew finds
    • Lucky (Adventurer): +20% double items
    • Seeker (Adventurer): +10% rare items
    • Swimmer (Pirate): +40% pickup range
    • Navigator (Pirate): +40% minimap range

    Key Insights

    • Adventurers cost less: 40 gold/hour vs 60 gold/hour, making them economical for long voyages
    • Private island taverns: Offer better encounter rates than port taverns for all crew types
    • Empire ships: Boarding Empire flagships has +15% unique crew chance with Rescuer crew
    • Scaling bonuses: Crew with "per sailor" or "per boarder" bonuses become more powerful with larger crews
    • Conditional bonuses: Some crew only activate at specific conditions (e.g., Helmsman at <50% durability)
    • Redhead is essential: For mixed Pirate/Military builds, Redhead resolves conflicts without taking a slot
    • Higher difficulty = better recruitment: Game mode difficulty affects boarding recruitment success rates
