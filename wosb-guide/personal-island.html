    Personal Island

    Overview

    Personal Islands are customizable bases where captains can construct buildings, store resources, produce goods, and launch expeditions. Each island serves as a strategic hub for crafting, storage, crew recruitment, and fast travel across the archipelago.

    Island Location Strategy

    Optimal Island Placement

    Before Level 12:

    Build your island near your spawn location with the closest Iron mine and Wood mine. This is a temporary setup while you're learning the game.

    After Level 12 (Recommended):

    Relocate your island and mines to the Black Waters region. Black water mines produce better quality resources, and you can access them starting at level 12.

    How to "Move": You don't physically move the island. Instead, build a new island in black waters, demolish your old mines, build new mines in black waters, and move all resources to your new main port.

    Work Resource System

    Work Resource is the core energy system that powers all island activities. It regenerates over time and can be replenished through various methods.

    Capacity & Regeneration

    Maximum: 275 units (based on island population)

    Base regeneration: 3 units/hour

    Increase maximum capacity with Castle (+50) or Tower (+25) decorations. Increase regeneration with Tavern (+1/hour) or Chapel decoration (+1/hour).

    Replenishment Methods

    Prisoners: 1 prisoner = 1 work resource

    Boarding crew: 1 musketeer/soldier/mercenary = 0.01 work resource

    Instant refill: 257 coins for full replenishment
