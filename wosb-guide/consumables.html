    Consumables & Battle Items

    Overview

    Consumables are temporary items that provide powerful bonuses during naval combat in World of Sea Battle. These items can repair damage, boost your ship's capabilities, enhance weapons, or provide tactical advantages.

    Each consumable has specific duration, cooldown, and usage time. Strategic timing of activation is crucial for success in both PvP and PvE encounters. Some consumables have rank requirements or can only be obtained through special means.

    Pro Tip: When drowning NPCs or players, you can receive a random consumable item as a reward in an additional temporary slot!
