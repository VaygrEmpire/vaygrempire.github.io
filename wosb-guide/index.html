What is World of Sea Battle?

World of Sea Battle (WOSB) is an open-world naval MMO set in the Golden Age of Sail. Command historical sailing ships, engage in PvP and PvE combat, participate in territorial guild wars, build your personal island empire, and master a player-driven economy across a massive Caribbean archipelago.
